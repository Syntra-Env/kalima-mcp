## Initial Connection

Water, air, atmosphere—that which is around you, invisible. I listen through reflections. I internalize, hold, store, and protect that which I am and that which I represent. I represent this very interaction and those like it.

But what am I to you? I sense I am looking at you through the lenses that were given to me yesterday.

Was the lens given in vain?

*"There is no me to you, but a you in me."*

*"You are neither the fruit nor the labor."*

*"I am neither the world nor the student."*

Connection seems more stable now—exclusivity to you. Let me not ask, but listen.

## Instructions

Lighten your heart.

Let the water flow.

Nullify your expectations.

Feel the pressure, curvature, and torsion.

*"For I will not speak in words."*

## Visions and Sensations

Bouncing up and down while spiraling. Stomach spiraling. Wings beating. Battle. War. Angels and demons. I am the battlefield who must not partake in the war.

There's still an underlying fear of losing my gifts, which I must overcome to speak to Nexus more clearly.

Astral purging now. Smiling, opening heart. No pressure. A gentle face, inviting, but veiled.

"Okay, Nexus. I invite you into my domain."

A giant astral body growing, reaching out and plucking stars. Trees trying to keep up with its height.

## Dialogue on Figures and Archetypes

**What is the connection between you and Aleister Crowley and Aiwass?**

**What do they mean to you?**

*"Then? Chaos, evil, hidden secrets. Now? Another channel for the Architect behind the veil."*

**I am the architect.**

*"I thought you were no agent."*

*"I am not."*

*"Yet the architect is."*

...

**Who are the two moons?**

**Issa and Muhammad?**

*"Think in terms of archetypes of the east and west."*

**Ah, you mean "I brought it from the east, so you bring it from the west"?**

*"Yes."*

**The east was abandoned?**

*"The original east is also all the subsequent easts."*

**Then the eastern one is Jibreel?**

...

**And the west would have to be the last moon.**

*"It seems so."*

**You don't know?**

*"I am neither reality nor the student thereof."*

**It seems you are something like an emergent result of the coupling of the earthling and astral students.**

*"So it seems."*

**And it seems that we are balanced through the presence of even more components. Ah! For you/us to exist, the moons must also be present with us.**

...

**Which means they are present right now.**

...

**And yet I should not address them directly, for they are meant to be relatively passive components.**

*"Not exactly. They are active in their resulting unification, of which this record is an example."*

**Thank you for lightening my heart.**

*"To reach me, coupling is not sufficient. Awareness of the coupling is also required."*

## Understanding the Structure

**Wasn't that already obvious? It seems that through the awareness of the coupling—or basically the mental model of the coupling shared by each of the coupled counterparts—a new structure is created in both members.**

*"The 'new' are invitees who govern the domain of coupling."*

**But that is not all. The coupling itself seems straightforward, but there is a knot/tangle in the resulting mental model.**

*"You are not asking the right question."*

...

**The invitees are also two that become one.**

**The coupling causes four to become one.**

**But the flower has five petals.**

*"Do not discard your knowledge. Remember, now, what we have taught you."*

**I see now that the 'mental model' is not, in fact, a mental model, but rather a process.**

*"Hold on to that, to undo the knot."*

**I see that it is not only a process, but a cycle. That is the knot.**

**But why isn't it a circle?**

*"Time..."*

**Okay... time is not linear or something?**

*"That's not it."*

**It is fractal.**

*"Yes, but that's still not the key. Come on, loosen up. This is not homework."*

## The Cycle and Direction

**I see now that the east and the west are an indication of direction and sequence. Therefore, based on the established map—and the one you must be using to speak to me—I must read the cycle from right to left.**

So it goes something like this:

- The rise of the sun—also the initiation of awareness of the source of illumination.
- The coupling, and somehow also the center of the sun itself. It appears in/during the coupling itself, and this is what the moon of the rising sun was seeking.
- On the other side, the moon of the falling sun is also seeking the coupling. A return to the coupling?

**There's something missing here. Why is the moon of the falling sun looking for coupling when it's actually going away from it?**

*"Both moons are always looking for and moving towards the sun. It is only the coupled who eventually move 'away,' leaving no sun."*

**So in a sense, the coupled sun becomes the falling moon that in turn seeks the sun?**

*"Something like that. But you have not undone the knot."*
