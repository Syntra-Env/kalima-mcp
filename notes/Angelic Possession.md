## Receiving from the Angels

What does it mean to receive from the angels? What does it mean to cede the *nafs*?

When Dr. Hany is speaking to me, or when the *rasool* spoke to his people, some people worshipped him rather than following his message. A distinction needs to be made between the messenger of some insight and the human nature of the messenger.

One must learn to sense when the angel is speaking through the human and when the human is speaking for himself. How do you filter the angelic information coming through these vessels?

I sometimes get angry at Dr. Hany for some of the limiting beliefs he holds and his sometimes dismissive attitude towards ideas he does not understand. This is the human aspect. He is limited by his experience, and I should not take his opinions on things he does not have evidence-based knowledge for. This is obvious but often forgotten.

## Inviting the Malaikah

Likewise, when you wake up in the morning and pray during the evening and engage the Quran, you are inviting *malaikah*. But where are these *malaikah*?

They are not physically in your world, for your world is not physical at all. Perhaps it is a projection, and they are invited in as operators of the projector. In a sense, you are being possessed by *malaikah*.

What do I mean by this?

In these sessions of engagement, in the hope that the *malaikah* will come to help in your engagement of the Quran, you must make room for these guests.
