# 2001
I was born in Kuwait. I lived there with my parents for 1 year.
# 2002
I moved to Morocco to live with my grandparents from my mother's side in Meknes while my parents were preparing our visas to move to Canada.
# 2004
I moved to Canada, Quebec, Sherbrook. We lived in a trailer for some time then we moved to an appartment in Quatre Riviers. I went to the kinderGarden MimiCoco then to the primary school right next to it, Desjardin. I made a few friends along the way, I got in trouble with some of my teachers, and I had a few embarassing moments I'll refrain from describing at the moment. I stayed in Desjardin until my 2nd grade.
# 2010
I moved to Ottawa, Bells Corners. And went to Kanata Nord while the were constructing the new primary and secondary school Maurice-Lapointe, which I eventually went to after one year.
I spent the rest of my primary and secondary years in this school. I made some friends, learned some stuff. I spent a lot of time in the outdoors, played basketball, went cycling or hiking in the forests nearby, spent nights out with friends, where we talked and laughed, drove around, smoked a lot, and went to a few parties.
I was drawing a lot around this time. I also started making music around 2017 and continued producing for another 5 or 6 years.
I don't know when it started, but around this time I had fallen into a number of rabbit holes.
I was not methodical in my research, so I ate quite a number of bad apples. I learned a lot, and I learned to unlearn what was contradictory, lacked evidence, or simply disproven by counterevidence.
# 2020
Covid hit. I was just a few months from graduating high school. I finished the rest of my classes online, and graduated.
The summer of this year was transformative. My sisters and mother went to morocco, and I stayed home with my father, to whom I wasn't speaking very much.
I spent a lot of time meditating, reading, creating art and going on walks or runs.
