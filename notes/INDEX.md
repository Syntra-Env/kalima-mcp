 # Quran Research Notes - Index

## Core Theoretical Framework

### [[NCU Framework and Interpretation]]
Formal theoretical framework for the Nafs-Centric Universe (NCU) - a model where each individual exists in their own bespoke reality orchestrated by malaikah. Explores the relationship between earthlings, malaikah, and multi-NCU narratives.

### [[Quran Self-Centric Interpretation]]
Interpretive methodology viewing Quranic narratives from the perspective of a single self, with other characters as either apparitions or representations of different stages of spiritual evolution.

### [[The Compatiblity of NCUs]]
Philosophical exploration of inter-universe compatibility within the NCU framework. Examines the tension between the imperative not to assume anything about other NCUs and the practical necessity of understanding afterlife characters, prophets, and angels. Explores locution, temporal paradoxes, and whether the Quran's coherence demands conceptual compatibility between different NCUs.

### [[Historical Context of Quranic Interpretation]]
Historical overview of Quranic interpretation methods, contrasting traditional authority-based approaches with modern intra-Quranic methodology. Highlights the work of Dr. Hany Achan and the organic Quranic methodology.

## Spiritual Practice & Experience

### [[Angelic Possession]]
Explores what it means to receive information from malaikah, how to distinguish between divine and human sources, and the practice of making room for angelic guidance during Quranic engagement.

### [[Discomfort and Spiritual Transformation]]
Examines the phenomenology of spiritual growth, reframing discomfort as the necessary process of transformation. Discusses patience, trust, and ceding the nafs to allow malaikah to work.

### [[Channeling Session]]
Experiential record of a channeling session exploring the coupling of earthling and astral students, archetypes of east and west, and the geometric/cyclic understanding of spiritual connection.

## Linguistic & Textual Analysis

### [[Quranic Word Root Analysis]]
Detailed linguistic analysis of Quranic roots (particularly ذ-ر and related terms), exploring metaphorical interpretations through the zipper model and the organic Quranic methodology framework.

### [[Al-Hijr]]
Reading notes on Surah Al-Hijr with focus on root-word analysis, the preposition "min," and identifying the community being addressed through linguistic clues.

### [[Brief Theological Notes]]
Short theological observations including Muhammad's prophethood, the concept of sayd (hunting for knowledge), and the meaning of haram.

### [[Story of Yusuf]]
Detailed transcript analysis of Surah Yusuf exploring NCU framework application, linguistic precision, priming effects, and challenging traditional tafseer interpretations. Covers conspiracy narrative, deception, ethical dilemmas, and cognitive biases in interpretation.

## Technical & Implementation

### [[Kalima - Quranic Knowledge Interface]]
Technical proposal for a methodology-aware knowledge interface for Quranic tadabbur. Explores geometric architectures (HUFD), certainty stratification, and active falsification tracking for building authoritative Quranic knowledge.
