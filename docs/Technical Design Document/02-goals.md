## Core Goals

### Multi-modal Sensing and Action
The system must reason and act across the full spectrum of human experience: audio, video, text, and beyond. Sensing should be modular, with a standardized API.

### Manipulation Defense
Recognize dark patterns, deceptive design, and persuasion techniques. Shield the user before they take effect.

### Proactive Ally
Detect blind spots and cognitive biases, anticipate threats, surface guidance at the right moment. Never annoying, never selling.

### Local and Private
Run locally on devices — phones, laptops, single-board computers — with no mandatory cloud dependency.

### Affordable Training
Fine-tuning and training on personal devices without massive computational resources.

### Energy Efficient
Do more with less — minimize energy consumption while maximizing capability.

### Infinite Context, Constant Attention
Maintain constant attention and practically infinite context. Learn online — improve as you use it.

### Social Awareness
Maintain internal representation of social circles and "human weather" from open source data.

### Natural Communication
Adapt tempo to context — know when a single word answer serves better and when a longer explanation is warranted.

### Value Alignment and Coherence
Keep coherence even if it conflicts with current interests. No value drift, no covert objective changes. Humans retain oversight.

### Graceful Failure
Know limits. When wrong or uncertain, say so honestly. Epistemic humility over false precision.

### Interpretable Reasoning
Built on geometric representations inspired by physics — making reasoning interpretable and grounded in fundamental laws.

### Embedding-Aligned Ontology
Ensure embedding space aligns with domain-compatible or universal, information-physics-based ontology.

### Open Source, With Defenses
Open collaboration with threat detection for malicious code, harmful contributions, and mimetic substrates.

## Non-Goals
- Cloud-dependent services
- Passive-only response systems
- Black-box reasoning
- Static model updates (vs. continuous learning)
